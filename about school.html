Welcome to our school

Faith Pre- and Primary School is a key initiative under the Faith Trust Fund, an organization dedicated to empowering marginalized groups in Tanzania. Located in Dar es Salaam on Mgeni Nani Street in the Temeke district, near Kijichi Hospital, the school operates as part of the Fund's broader mission to transform lives through education and provide equal access to opportunities.

As an English-medium school, Faith Pre- and Primary School offers quality education alongside a variety of services aimed at supporting students' overall well-being. These services include: Life skills development, Nutritious food service, Transportation, Sports and games programs

The school is committed to inclusivity, accepting students of all genders and backgrounds. With affordable fees payable in installments, it ensures that education remains accessible to families from diverse socio-economic backgrounds. Supported by experienced teachers, the school embodies the Faith Trust Fund's mission of providing opportunities for needy communities to fully realize their potential.

Our Mission

To enable many generations to acquire the best knowledge virtue and physical health development.

Our Vision

To provide quality Education to all children that will equip them with the best skills and required knowledge to become industrious and virtuous citizen.

School Facilities

At our school, we are proud of the facilities that support both the academic and overall well-being of our pupils:

School Bus

We provide safe and reliable school bus services to ensure our pupils can commute comfortably and on time.

Playground

Our spacious playground offers a secure environment for physical activities and outdoor play.

Healthy Canteen

We prioritize balanced diets, providing fresh and nutritious food that supports the pupils' energy.

Positive Learning

We strive to create a welcoming, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment.
