Welcome to our School

FAITH Pre and Primary School is a school that provides education services from Day care, Nursery, to Primary level. The school is located in Kijichi area, Mgeni nani street, next to the government hospital in Temeke District, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. The school is registered and recognized by the Ministry of Education in Tanzania, with registration number EM 19906. Services provided by the school include; Education, Food, and Transport. The school considers the importance of providing quality education to its students, from day care to primary education. Students have the opportunity to receive education carefully prepared by experienced teachers.

Our Core Values

Education (Elimu)

We are committed to providing quality education that fosters intellectual growth and curiosity in every child. Our goal is to equip pupils with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed academically and in life.

Guidance and Formation (Malezi)

Beyond academics, we emphasize character formation and personal development. Our staff provides supportive guidance, helping pupils develop good judgment, emotional intelligence, and leadership skills, shaping them into well-rounded individuals.

Ethics (Maadili)

Integrity and moral responsibility are at the heart of everything we do. We instill strong ethical values in our pupils, encouraging them to make positive choices, respect others, and contribute to society in meaningful ways.
